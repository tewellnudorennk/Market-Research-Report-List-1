<p><p>The flame retardant polypropylene (PP) granules market is segmented into two primary types: halogen type and halogen-free type. Halogen-type flame retardants contain elements like bromine or chlorine, providing effective fire resistance but raising environmental and health concerns. In contrast, halogen-free type flame retardants, often made from phosphorus, nitrogen, or inorganic materials, offer a safer alternative with lesser toxicity and environmental impact. Both types cater to diverse industries, balancing performance and safety based on specific application requirements.</p></p>
