Descriptive analytics focuses on historical data to understand past trends and outcomes in healthcare. Predictive analytics uses statistical models and algorithms to forecast future health trends and patient outcomes based on existing data. Prescriptive analytics goes a step further by recommending actions to optimize healthcare decisions, such as treatment plans or resource allocation, thereby enhancing patient care and operational efficiency.</p></p>
