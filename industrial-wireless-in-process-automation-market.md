<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Industrial Wireless in Process Automation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wireless Local Area Network (WLAN)</li><li>Wireless Personal Area Network (WPAN)</li><li>Satellite (GNSS)</li><li>Low-Power Wide-Area Network (LPWAN)</li><li>Cellular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial wireless in process automation encompasses several types of communication technologies. Wireless Local Area Networks (WLAN) enable high-speed data transfer over short ranges, ideal for localized operations. Wireless Personal Area Networks (WPAN) connect devices within limited distances, suitable for personal devices and sensors. Satellite (GNSS) technology provides global positioning and connectivity support for remote operations. Low-Power Wide-Area Networks (LPWAN) offer long-range communication with low energy consumption, ideal for IoT applications. Cellular networks facilitate robust, wide-area connectivity for mobile industrial applications.</p></p>
