<p><p>The on-load tap changing transformer market is categorized into vacuum and oil-immersed types. Vacuum transformers operate by having the tap-changing mechanism in a vacuum environment, minimizing contamination and enhancing reliability. In contrast, oil-immersed transformers utilize oil for insulation and cooling, providing efficient heat dissipation and maintaining performance under varying loads. Both types serve critical roles in optimizing voltage levels in power distribution networks, catering to different operational requirements and environmental conditions, thus influencing their demand and application.</p></p>
