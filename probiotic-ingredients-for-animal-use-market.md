<p><p>The probiotic ingredients for animal use market is categorized into three main types: bacteria, yeast, and spore formers. Bacteria, such as Lactobacillus and Bifidobacterium, support gut health and digestion in animals. Yeast, like Saccharomyces cerevisiae, enhance nutrient absorption and promote a balanced gut microbiome. Spore formers, including Bacillus species, are resilient and can survive harsh conditions, providing stable probiotic benefits. These ingredients are vital in animal nutrition, improving overall health, immunity, and productivity in livestock and pets.</p></p>
