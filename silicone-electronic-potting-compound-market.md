<p><ul><li>Condensed Type</li><li>Addition</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The silicone electronic potting compound market can be categorized into two main types: condensed type and addition type. Condensed type compounds cure through moisture absorption from the environment, resulting in a faster setting time but generally lower heat resistance. In contrast, addition type compounds cure through an addition reaction, offering superior thermal stability and mechanical properties, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Both types are essential for protecting electronics from environmental factors and enhancing their longevity and reliability.</p></p>
