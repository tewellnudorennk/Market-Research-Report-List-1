<p><strong>The Voice Prosthesis Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Indwelling Voice Prosthesis</li><li>Non-indwelling Voice Prosthesis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The voice prosthesis market consists of two main types: indwelling and non-indwelling voice prostheses. Indwelling voice prostheses are surgically implanted and can remain in place for extended periods, allowing for continuous use and requiring minimal maintenance. In contrast, non-indwelling voice prostheses are typically removable and require periodic replacement by the user. They offer more flexibility but may necessitate more frequent care and handling. Both types aim to restore voice function for individuals who have undergone laryngectomy or suffer from voice disorders.</p></p>
